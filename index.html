About

I’m a video editor with 2 years of experience and a background in animation and multimedia, currently shaping content at Blkbox.ai.

My day-to-day spans cutting raw gameplay into fast-paced, hook-driven edits, syncing action to sound, adding motion graphics and on-screen UI, and delivering ad creatives tuned for retention and conversion.
